INTERNATIONAL STUDY OF WORK AND FAMILY

Welcome to the website for the International Study of Work and Family (ISWAF). The primary objective of ISWAF is to extend existing cross-national work-family research by examining the positive aspects of the work-family interface as well as issues associated with work-family boundary management. Another objective is to nurture cross-national work-family research through collaboration with colleagues across the world. Through this website, our aim is to provide news and information about the project and resources for the global community of work and family scholars.
